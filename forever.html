THIS GAME IS INTENDED TO WORK ON DESKTOPS AND CERTAIN MOBILE ASPECT RATIOS ONLY. IT IS NOT RESPONSIVE AND DOES NOT RESIZE THE LAYOUT AUTOMATICALLY.

FOREVER MISSION & GAME

Rivian has established "Forever," a philanthropic mission dedicated to addressing the climate crisis and preserving biodiversity. By contributing 1% of its equity into Forever, the company hopes to focus on high-impact climate initiatives with an emphasis on preserving and restoring wildlands, waterways and oceans. The company also plans to reduce its need for land and resources through more sustainable and efficient consumption and will engage employees, community members and partners to maximize its collective impact.

To promote this mission, I created the "Forever Game" - which only takes forever if you're REALLY BAD. It's essentially just a matching game. You'll get it once you start playing.

HOW TO PLAY:

Start by selecting any of the 12 tiles. Once the tile flips, you'll see a Rivian vehicle in a distinct color. Now keep flipping tiles until you find a match. Restart by refreshing the page. Have fun!
